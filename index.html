GIVE BACK TO THE FUTURE

Three Tree Montessori School's biggest event of the year is taking place on Friday, April 21st at the Georgetown Ballroom. Give 'Back to the Future' is an opportunity for us to celebrate who we are, all we have accomplished, and raise funds that will allow us to continue to implement a high quality Montessori philosophy in our classrooms.

GET INVOLVED

Three Tree Montessori School’s 14th Annual Auction would not be possible without the substantial support of our community. There are still many ways you can get involved.


Volunteer

The 2017 Auction Committee is always seeking support from our parents and community friends as we plan this event. Please see the TTMS Tuesday Memo for an upcoming meeting date and time. We will also be seeking volunteers to help with decoration-making and day-of-event tasks. If you are interested in volunteering for this event, please contact Rachel Haas using the contact information below.

Donate Wine

We are currently seeking wine (red, white, and/or bubbly) for our wine toss. If you would like to contribute, please bring your bottle(s) valued at $15 or more to the school and complete a procurement form which can be found at the front desk or right here.

Sponsor

We are looking for local businesses to sponsor our auction, which help to underwrite our event costs. In return we offer a generous marketing package including social media blasts and recognition on this webpage! For information on levels of sponsorship, specific marketing package benefits, or additional information please contact Rachel Haas at the information listed below.
