Aspen-Red Cedar Class Project: Floating Hearts

Take home this truly unique piece of art, created by the Aspen and Red Cedar classrooms. The Aspen lower elementary students worked together to handcraft the paper used in this project! Once complete, their paper was passed off to Red Cedar. These primary students first traced heart shapes onto the handmade paper using a special metal insert, then painted their personal designs using oil pastels. Finally, the collection of hearts was set in a stunning floating glass frame.
